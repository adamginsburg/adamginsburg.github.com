Please email Adam Ginsburg if you have any problems using this form, or if you would like to free up a night that you have previously reserved.

The names of students observing for class will be shown in blue. The names of any other observers and of students observing for fun will be shown in red. If you are observing for fun, please select 'Other' under 'Class/Function'.

ASTR 3510 students observing for classwork have priority over all other observers. If a student group needs to observe during a time slot otherwise reserved by a lower priority user, please email Adam and he will manually change the calendar and notify the other observers that they have been bumped.
