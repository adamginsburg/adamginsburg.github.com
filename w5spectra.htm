W5 Outflows Paper

Contours overlaid on NEWFIRM H2, Spitzer IRAC 8 micron, MIPS 24 micron, and BGPS 1.1mm grayscale images are shown below. The H2, 8 micron, and 24 micron images are displayed from 0 to 2.5 ???, 700 to 15000, and 650 to 5000 MJy/sr in inverse log scale, and the BGPS from -0.1 to 1.0 Jy/beam in linear scale.

The contours are red and blue shifted emission corresponding to their colors. The velocities integrated over are listed in the image titles. The contours are at levels .5,1,1.5,2,3,4,5,6,8,10,12,14,16,18,20,25,30,35,40,45,50,60,70,80,90,100 K km/s.

The red and blue X's mark the approximate locations of the outflows, which do not always correspond to the peak of the integrated emission. The orange X's and labels identify likely candidates for the outflow sources; most are associated with 8 and 24 micron point sources.
