Collaborators


A list of people I work with, not including former advisors listed on my previous work page.

John Bally is my advisor. His work, and mine, is on star formation. He is PI of the Bolocam Galactic Plane Survey and is generally interested in massive star formation and (proto)stellar outflows.

Jeremy Darling works with formaldehyde a lot and is on my thesis committee.

Nathan Smith works on LBVs such as Eta Carinae and once was a postdoc at CU. I almost worked with him on Eta Car, and we are collaborating on P Cygni work.

Guy Stringfellow looks at novae. We are working together on P Cygni.

Jonathan Williams is at the University of Hawaii and is a member of the Bolocam Galactic Plane Survey, and we have worked together on James Clerk Maxwell Telescope observing.

Josh Walawender did his graduate research under John Bally, so I'm working with him to help finish off some work he began as a grad student at CU but did not finish.

James Aguirre is responsible for the Bolocam data pipeline that I have worked on.

Cara Battersby is the other grad student in the star formation group at CU.

Miranda Nordhaus is a grad student at the University of Texas. She works on BGPS.

Meredith Drosback was a postdoc at CU and is now at Virginia. She works on BGPS.
