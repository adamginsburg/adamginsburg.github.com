Another Different Approach

The sources selected below are those Josh extracted apertures for.
The most interesting common feature is a red excess in nearly all stars.
Plotted are the ratios between the central star and its surrounding aperture. I think the ratio is a better measure than the difference.
The grid plots are the central 25 points around the star center divided by each corner at a distance of 3 pixels in the x and y directions. These show whether the points within the aperture are likely to be consistent.
