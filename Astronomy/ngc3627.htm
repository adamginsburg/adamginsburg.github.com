NGC 3627


A galaxy located at J2000 coordinates of Declination 12 59 30 Right Ascension 11 20 15




I reduced VLA archival data for this galaxy during my REU in the summer of 2006.
While at the REU, I learned to reduce VLA data using AIPS, the Astronomical Image Processing System
Thanks to some of AIPS peculiar abilities and difficulties, I also learned a little bit about directly editing postscript files, which allowed me to make some relatively unique diagrams.
